SHANGHAI

Shanghai is home to 26.32 billion people, which makes it one of the most populated cities in the world. Located on the east coast of China, Shanghai is known as one of the global financial hubs. In Shanghai, you will have a chance to see numerous skyscrapers and observe the fast-paced lifestyle of contemporary China. Our Shanghai program provides you with the opportunity to visit the heart of the city—the Bund, with its futuristic skyline arisen across the Hungpu River. Meanwhile, traditional Chinese architecture and lifestyle can also be seen within the city. For instance, Yu Garden with its magnificent ancient buildings can tell you a lot about the history of Shanghai.

SHENZHEN

Located in the southeast of China, Shenzhen serves as a bridge connecting Hong Kong to Mainland China. It is one of the cities where China’s reform and opening-up started in the 1970s. Shenzhen features a large number of contemporary buildings, amusement parks, and shopping destinations. If you aim to understand modern China better and enjoy tropical climate and vegetation, our Shenzhen program would be an ideal choice
